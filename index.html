lost items

Get information about your lost items.

Where are they kept?

Your items are stored in the Reproductions Office for a limited time and then transferred to the Student Affairs Office.

How much time do I have to claim them?

Your items are kept for 30 days before being put up for sale on this same website.

How do I report if I found or lost an item?

You can do it in person by going to Carlitos or approaching a member of the student council.
